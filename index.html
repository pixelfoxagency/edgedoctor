About

My name is Ken Arno, known as the Edge Doctor.

For over thirty years I have received on-site training at several renowned factories such as the Aris Eckhart Factory in Ohio to master the art of blade sharpening. I pride myself on delivering the highest level of craftsmanship for my customers. I care about the details and integrity of the blades I sharpen so I choose to use hand sharpening techniques and modern equipment.

Unlike some industry providers I never use automatic sharpening equipment which erodes blades and damages the longevity of your tools. I value my long term customer relationships and will use my extensive experience to guarantee the best possible service and my work.

I look forward to meeting you and thank you for giving me the opportunity to service your tools.
